Pomodoro Timer

25:00

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that helps people stay focused by working in short, dedicated bursts. Instead of long, tiring work sessions, this technique encourages concentrating on a task for a set period, making it easier to maintain productivity and avoid distractions.

In this method, a single work session lasts 25 minutes, during which you focus entirely on the task at hand. After the session, a short break can be taken to rest and refresh the mind. The timer can be started, paused, or reset, giving users control over their workflow and allowing flexibility depending on their schedule or focus level.

By using a 25-minute timer with simple controls, the Pomodoro Technique turns large tasks into manageable chunks. Even without tracking multiple cycles, this approach helps maintain concentration, manage time effectively, and improve overall productivity.
